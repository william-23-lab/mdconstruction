About MD Construction & Estate Limited

We are a passionate team of builders, engineers, and designers who believe that every structure tells a story. From foundations to finishing, we pour dedication into every project we undertake — because your vision deserves nothing less than excellence.

Our Story

We Build More Than Structures

MD Construction and Estate Limited was established on 11th April 2022 as a subsidiary of Maxidivine Limited Company to expand into construction and real estate development. Leveraging Maxidivine’s experience and resources in building materials, block production, sanitary ware, and tiles, the company enjoys direct access to essential construction materials, reducing procurement delays and improving project efficiency. Since its establishment, MD Construction and Estate Limited has been actively engaged in construction and real estate development across Ghana, contributing to the growth of the housing and infrastructure sectors.
